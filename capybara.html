Capybara Fun Facts

Capybaras are semi-aquatic and will spend most of their time in dense vegetation around rivers, lakes, ponds, marshes and swamps.

Capybaras and guinea pigs are closely related. They are both members of the Caviidae family.

Capybaras can weigh more than 100 lbs and grow to be 4 feet long and 2 feet tall.
